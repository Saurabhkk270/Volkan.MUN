About Us

Welcome to Volkan Model United Nations, where young leaders converge to debate, negotiate, and solve global issues. Located in the heart of Faridabad, we bring you an experience like no other.

Our mission is to ignite the fire of diplomacy in every delegate, fostering critical thinking and public speaking skills that will last a lifetime.
